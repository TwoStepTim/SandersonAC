Sponsors

Why Become A Sponsor

Sponsoring Sanderson provides your brand with exceptional visibility and engagement opportunities. You'll connect with a diverse audience, gain exclusive networking opportunities, and showcase your products or services to a receptive market. Support our mission of innovation and collaboration while driving success together.

Red Tier Sponsorship - $500

Our Red Tier Sponsorship is designed for those looking to make a significant impact with a smaller investment. At $500, this basic tier offers your brand essential visibility and recognition at our events. You'll be featured in our promotional materials and have the opportunity to connect with our community, helping you build valuable relationships and enhance your brand's presence.

Blue Tier Sponsorship - $1000

The Blue Tier Sponsorship is perfect for those who want to take their brand engagement to the next level. For $1000, you'll receive all the benefits of the Red Tier, plus enhanced visibility through premium placement in our marketing materials and event signage. This tier also includes additional networking opportunities, giving you greater access to our diverse and dynamic audience.

White Tier Sponsorship - $2000

Our White Tier Sponsorship is the ultimate investment in visibility and engagement. At $2000, this top-tier package offers maximum brand exposure with prominent placement in all promotional materials, exclusive networking opportunities, and the ability to showcase your products or services at our events. This level of sponsorship ensures your brand stands out and is recognized as a key supporter of our mission and community.
