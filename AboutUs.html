Our Commitment

Our Programs

We offer a wide range of sports programs designed to cater to the diverse interests and talents of our students. These include:

  • Fall Sports: Football, Soccer, Cross Country, Volleyball, and Cheerleading.
  • Winter Sports: Basketball, Wrestling, Indoor Track, and Swimming.
  • Spring Sports: Baseball, Softball, Track and Field, Tennis, and Lacrosse.

Achievements

Our teams have a proud history of success, with numerous district, regional, and state championships. We celebrate the hard work and dedication of our athletes and coaches who have made these accomplishments possible.

Community Involvement

The Sanderson High School Athletic Club is deeply committed to giving back to the community. We organize various events and outreach programs to engage with and support our local community, fostering a sense of pride and connection.

Join Us

Whether you’re a student interested in joining a team, a parent looking to support your child’s athletic journey, or a community member wanting to get involved, we welcome you to be a part of the Sanderson High School Athletic Club. Together, we can achieve great things.
