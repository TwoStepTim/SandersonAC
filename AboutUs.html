About Us

About Us

The Sanderson High School Athletic Club is dedicated to fostering a spirit of sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence among our student-athletes. We strive to support and enhance the athletic experience for all students, encouraging them to achieve their personal best both on and off the field. Join us in celebrating the hard work, dedication, and achievements of our athletes. Go Spartans!

Membership

Join the Sanderson community today and experience the benefits of our exclusive membership. Our members enjoy access to premium facilities, special events, and discounts on programs. Whether you're a student, alumni, or community member, becoming a part of Sanderson means you’re supporting a tradition of excellence and fostering a vibrant, connected community. Sign up now to be a part of something special!

Concessions

Our concessions stand is your go-to spot for delicious snacks and refreshing beverages during games and events. From classic popcorn and hotdogs to healthier options and gourmet treats, we’ve got something for everyone. Your purchases support our teams and programs, helping us provide the best experience for our athletes and fans. Come hungry, leave happy!

Team Parent

Team parents are the backbone of our sports programs, providing essential support to coaches and players. From organizing team activities and managing communications to coordinating travel and snacks, team parents ensure everything runs smoothly. If you’re passionate about supporting our teams and creating a positive environment for our athletes, consider volunteering as a team parent. Your involvement makes a big difference!
